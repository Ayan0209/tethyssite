TETHYS is a consortium of highly specialized professionals that have previously served in the United Nations, The World Bank, Governments at the National, State and District level, Bilateral Institutions and International organizations and have now come together with a mission to give back to society a more sustainable and healthy living environment. The responsible business practice at TETHYS offers integrated solutions on strategy, last mile delivery, integrating programmes with the government, and developing partnerships for convergence of efforts to provide a multiplier effect. We offer evidence-based advice to match recurrent development problems with proven strategies, methods and tools to achieve equitable solutions. Our programme management initiative provides us an opportunity to engage closely with public policy and decision making process. An indication of our programme management expertise comes from our initiatives on management of risk of varied nature including climate change.

TETHYS takes up quantitative and qualitative (participatory) research, both fundamental and applied, as a part of knowledge-based or customized services. The in-house data analytic center and network with the field resources enables us to provide single-window services to a largescale data based research. TETHYS has proficiency in developing knowledge based products encompassing sector specific know-how as well as data management and disseminating this knowledge through capacity building services to a variety of organizations. The perfect interweave between these competencies enable TETHYS to develop a self-sufficient system for integrated support to organizations working on development issues. Tethys also provides a credible platform facilitating companies, donor agencies, philanthropists and foundations to achieve their specific CSR goals and make informed decisions that translate into purposeful action and large-scale social impact.
